Preparations


Step 1

Put your butter in a bowl and mix.

Caution:When mixing,mix in a single direction till you are done and ready to bake

Step 2

Add your sugar and vanilla flavour then mix till you get a fluffy butter

Step 3

In another bowl break in your eggs and whisk with the the spatula/fork till you get a foamy mixture.

Step 4

In another bowl sift in your flour and baking powder.

Step 5

Add your baking powder and flour mixture into the batter and mix.

Step 6

mix your batter with the eggs throughly.

Step 7

Add your mixture into the cup cake pan.

Caution:Add cupcake lylon into the cupcake pan before baking.

Step 8

Bake for 30 minute under {350 degree}


Enjoy Your Cup-cake
